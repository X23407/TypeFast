FAQs

Why Should I use this website?
Answer : We provide variety of features in our app which you can't find in any other app. We also have very interactive and good communit that may help you.

Is the app free to use?
Answer : Yes, The website is completely free to use without any subscription and adds!

Does TypeFAST support different practice modes?
Answer : Yes! You can choose from multiple modes like numbers, punctuation, code, timed tests, and even a chill relax mode for smooth practice.

Can I track my progress?
Answer : Of course! TypeFAST shows your WPM, accuracy, errors, and overall performance so you can see your improvement over time.

Can beginners use this website?
Answer : Absolutely! TypeFAST is designed for everyone — whether you're learning typing from scratch or trying to break your speed records.
